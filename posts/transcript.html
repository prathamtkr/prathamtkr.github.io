Grading System & CGPA Calculation

The medium of instruction is English.

A letter grade on a ten-point scale is awarded to a student in a course based on the total marks obtained from Continuous Assessment Tests and Final Assessment Test.

Letter Grade S A B C D E F N
Grade Point 10 9 8 7 6 5 0 0
  • F Grade: Failed in a Course
  • N Grade: Absent / Debarred In Final Assessment Test / Course Incomplete
  • P Grade: Passed in a Pass / Fail Course
  • U Grade: Successfully completed an Audit Course

Grades S, A, B, C, D, E, P and U indicate successful completion of the Course.

CGPA Calculation:

CGPA is obtained by dividing the sum of the product of Grade Point (GPI) earned and Credits (CI) of a registered course for 'n' number of courses by the total number of credits registered.

\[ \text{CGPA} = \frac{\sum_{i=1}^{n} (C_i \times GP_i)}{\sum_{i=1}^{n} C_i} \]

Bridge Course, Non-Credit Course, Pass Course (P Grade), Audit Course (U Grade) are not counted for the calculation of GPA or CGPA.
